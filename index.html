Your body mass index (BMI) is 21.64
The information you have provided indicates that you are in the healthy weight range for your height. It is important to maintain a healthy weight to decrease the risk of heart disease, some cancers and Type 2 Diabetes. To maintain a healthy weight ensure you continue to eat a healthy, balanced diet and take part in regular physical activity. It is recommended that young people are active for at least 60 minutes per day and adults are active for a minimum of 30 minutes a day, 5 times a week.

Your BMI percentile shows how your BMI compares to other young people who were the same age and gender as you (in 1990).
For example if your BMI is 24 and is on the 80th percentile for your age and gender, that means your BMI is in the top 20
for weight and that there are 80 young people with a lower BMI.

What are the centile ranges?
BMI 1st to 4th percentile: Underweight
BMI 5th to 84th percentile: Healthy Weight
BMI 85th to 94th percentile: Overweight
BMI 95th to 100th percentile: Obese

Therefore, if your BMI is in either in the top 15% or bottom 4%, you are likely to have health risks due to your weight.
